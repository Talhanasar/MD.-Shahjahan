## Color Palette Used

### Primary Colors:
- **Teal**: `teal-50` to `teal-950`
- **Green**: `green-50` to `green-900`
- **Stone**: `stone-50` to `stone-900`

### Accent Colors:
- **Red**: `red-100` to `red-600` (for specific highlights)
- **Orange**: `orange-500` (for contact icon gradient)

### Gradient Combinations:
1. **Teal to Green**: `from-teal-500 to-green-500` (vibrant icons)
2. **Teal Monochrome**: `from-teal-800 to-teal-900` (buttons)

4. **Red to Orange**: `from-red-500 to-orange-500` (contact icon)
5. **Text Gradients**: `from-teal-900 via-teal-800 to-green-800` (titles)
